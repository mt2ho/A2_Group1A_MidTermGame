How to Play

Welcome to Planet Amtcha 🌿☕

You’ve started your first shift at an alien café on Planet Amtcha. The locals are friendly, but their words come out in strange, jumbled sounds. Your goal is to understand their orders and keep them happy.

- Watch the customer’s order bubble appear and listen carefully to what they say.

- If the message is too unclear, press “Huh?” to hear a slightly clearer version. It won’t give you the full answer, but it might help.

- Once you think you know the order, tap “Okay!” to open the POS system.

- Choose the right Temperature, Base, and any Flavor or Topping.

- Press Submit to serve the drink and see how satisfied your customer is.

Finish your shift with a satisfaction rating above 50% to move on to the next day. The better you do, the closer you get to mastering the language of Planet Amtcha.
